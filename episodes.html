These participants were presented with three facts that they haven't heard before and stated their honest opinions for the first time in front of the camera. It's a topic Americans may be affected by, rather you're the affected or you witness the affection; the obesity epidemic began in the U.S in the 1980's, there has been a sharp increase since.

The participants were not asked questions. I did not want to format their answer rather it be in a positive or negative perspective. Questions were not created in order to receive pure opinion.
