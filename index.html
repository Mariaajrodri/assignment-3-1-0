Existing Opinions is dedicated to American voices, specifically the ones that aren't heard. The voices in this country that are heard are predominantly from politicians and celebrities.

This project is meant to voice opinions, to demonstrate the importance of a citizen that is not at a well-known level like a politician or a celebrity. You will notice that most of the participants are college students even though the project is not based on young adults, however, Millennials and Generation Z have a open mindedness that the above generations to do not have due to the fact that citizens over the age of 40 have a specific way they were raised. If you have any type of opinions, questions, ideas; submit them to the comment section below.

The format

The topics are not predominately following the itch of Americans but is following what has impacts on our lives. They are chosen randomly per week and per who would like to participate biweekly. The recording of the videos is quite simple. Before recording the topic is presented to the participant to make sure they are okay discussing the topic. After the topic is presented to them with the topic, the recording starts, and the participant is told the first fact of the topic. Three facts or questions will be told to them with their response after each one. In no way were answers cut or reconstructed.
